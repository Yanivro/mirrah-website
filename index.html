why I built this

I have ADHD. I spent years stuck in my head — making up stories, overthinking every text. while dating, hard conversations would send me reaching for my phone to calm down. but I'd end up writing texts I didn't want to write, doing things I didn't want to do.

I learned everything — my patterns, my habits, all of it. knowing didn't help me act differently in the moment.

then I learned to listen to my body. it changed my life. the phone will always be in our hands — so I wanted to put something good in it. a small choice that makes room for a bigger one.
